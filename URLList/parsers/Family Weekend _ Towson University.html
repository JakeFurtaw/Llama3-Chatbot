Family Weekend

Each fall, Family Weekend gives parents, siblings, grandparents, and the whole family the perfect opportunity to get to know the TU community and spend quality time together.

Family Weekend 2024: Save the Date!

Family Weekend 2024 will be held Friday, October 4 to Sunday, October 6. We look forward to seeing all of our Tiger families! The official schedule and event registration pages will be posted by August 26, 2024.

The new Hampton Inn in Towson, Maryland has TU rates for orientation, Family Weekend, and any visits to campus. Use the TU booking link to reserve a room. For more hotel options, you can also visit the Local Accommodations & Attractions page.
